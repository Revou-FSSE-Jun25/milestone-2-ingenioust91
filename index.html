Behind the Office Pup



Revofun is a small indie game developer dedicated to bringing short, fun, and easy-to-play browser games to busy professionals.


We believe that a quick laugh and a few minutes of distraction can reset your mind and help you tackle the rest of your day with more focus. Our mission is to create simple, stress-free online games that you can enjoy in between meetings, during coffee breaks, or whenever you need a moment to breathe.
